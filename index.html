Why Choose Leaf Landscape Supply?

Wholesale & Retail Nursery & Landscape Supplier in Austin, TX

Leaf Landscape Supply is a wholesale and retail nursery based in Austin, TX, with two locations at 5700 Hwy 290 W and 13292 Pond Springs Rd. We offer a wide range of products, from bulk landscaping materials to houseplants and unique botanical finds, making us your one-stop shop for all things green. Our doors are open to the public Monday through Saturday from 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM.

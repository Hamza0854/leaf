Rooted in Texas, Growing With Landscapers Since 1983

More Than a Supplier, Your Landscape Partner

At Leaf Landscape Supply, we go beyond selling plants and materials. Since 1983, our mission has been to support Texas landscapers with unmatched plant diversity, responsive service, and a team that knows your success is our success. Whether you're building residential gardens or large-scale commercial projects, we're here with the products, knowledge, and commitment to help you deliver.

History of Leaf

Leaf Landscape Supply started as Emerald Garden in 1983. It was owned by Luke Hogan and Trey Wyatt. In August of 2014 Brad Seever purchased ownership from Luke Hogan and the company was rebranded Leaf Landscape Supply with a mission to professionally service Texas landscapers with the highest quality landscape materials.

Our Mission

At Leaf Landscape Supply our mission is simple: to make our customers' visions a reality through superior quality, an excellent selection, and individualized customer service. We offer a full range of services for our professional landscape contractors

Brad Seever

A Round Rock, TX native. He graduated from Elgin High School and Texas A&M University. He began his career in high school working as general labor for Burt Thomas at Turkey Creek Farms, in Pflugerville, TX and hasn't left the nursery business since. After graduating with a B.S. in Horticulture from Texas A&M, he worked for Skinner Nurseries as a Purchasing Scout, covering the southern United States, building relations and procuring product from premier nurseries throughout the country. Brad began his management career with Skinner Nurseries in 2005, opening the Austin, TX distribution center. In 2008 he joined Newton Nursery as General Manager for Austin, TX branch.

Trey Wyatt

Roy L Wyatt III (Trey), graduated from Travis High School and Texas State University with a B.S. in Agriculture. He opened AAA Grass and Landscape with Luke Hogan in 1983, operating for 17 years when sold in May of 2000 to Turfgrass America, now King Ranch Turf. Trey founded Emerald Garden Water Gardens and Nursery with Luke Hogan in 1990, and founded Cedar Creek Tree Farms in 2001, near Cedar Creek, TX, that continues operating today. In August of 2014 Trey joined with Brad Seever, who mastered a new direction for Leaf Landscape Supply, which is a continuing success story. Trey draws support from his wife, Melissa, daughter, Hannah and son, Quatro. He enjoys farming, raising cattle, snow skiing, and bike riding.
